Extract data from graph images

Graph Value Extractor turns points and curves in chart screenshots into reusable X/Y values. It runs locally in your browser, supports numeric and date axes, linear and logarithmic scales, multiple data series, and CSV export.

Open the graph extraction tool

How it works

  1. Upload a PNG or JPG graph screenshot.
  2. Mark two reference points on each axis and enter their values.
  3. Click points or curves on the graph.
  4. Review the calculated X/Y values and download a CSV file.

Private, browser-based processing

Your graph image and coordinate calculations stay in your browser. You can save the project as JSON and continue later without uploading the source image to this website.
